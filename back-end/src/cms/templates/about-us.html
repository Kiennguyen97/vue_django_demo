Our Story

Simple, Practical and Great Value bathroom products are at the heart of what makes us successful. Founded in the regional city of Whanganui in 2000, Newtech has established itself as a fast-growing leader in the bathroomware space in New Zealand.

We believe your bathroom is the most important space in your home. This is where you spend time alone; relaxing, making plans and dreaming of the future. Your bathroom should reflect who you are and what you are about.

So, its important that you make your bathroom decisions wisely. Our commitment to innovation, to improvement and, most of all, delivering great value should put Newtech in the ‘must consider’ category for your bathroom.

We have a huge range of baths, toilets, vanities and other bathroom essentials available in our warehouse for immediate dispatch. These are available to purchase from most bathroom merchants in New Zealand. Check out our list of stockists here to find your nearest supplier of Newtech bathroomware products or feel free to drop into our fantastic showroom in Penrose, Auckland.
