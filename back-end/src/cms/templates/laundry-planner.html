4 Easy Steps with design@home

Design your new LAUNDRY with design@home online planner

01
Start

To make your work easier, we have predefined some standard room shapes for you. For these you only need to enter the specific room dimensions, then you can start planning. If your room has a different shape, please select "Free Room". Optionally, you can load a saved plan or use a furnished room template.

02
Build

To further adapt the interior design to your expectations and wishes, you can add finishing touches to the walls, doors, floor and other elements. Click on an element in the room and select from the left column the appropriate property you want to modify.

03
Furnish

Select your desired products from the Laundry catalogue displayed on the left hand side. You can add any product to your room by simply clicking on it or using drag & drop.

04
View

When you have finished planning your laundry you can save, print or revisit your individual plan at any stage by reloading and editing. Once your are happy with your design, simply contact us at design@newtech.co.nz to make your laundry a reality.
